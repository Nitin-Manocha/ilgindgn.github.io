About Me

Research

My research focuses on designing data-driven and adaptive approaches with strong theoretical and practical performance guarantees for sequential decision-making processes with partial observation and strategic agents. Motivated by complex and unexplored real-world operations management (OM) problems (ranging from supply chain to sustainability analytics), I bridge the classical OM theory with sequential learning theory by exploiting machinery from statistics, machine learning, optimization, and principal-agent theory.
